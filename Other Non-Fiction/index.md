# How Do We Change The World?
## A requiem against inactivity, complacency, and lack of community

Oftentimes I find myself talking to people who yearn for the ability to affect the world around them for the better. Whether they wish to create a space for their ilk to exit within, or if they want to influence an artistic scene to some degree, I have even found myself talking to people who wish to politically influence the world. These people speak about their dreams to change the world, their longing to participate in something bigger than themselves, yet they do not participate in the fundamental acts of creation that would allow them to leave their mark on the world.
