# Pho Hanoi

Pho Hanoi serves massive portions of delicious pho that are perfectly spiced. You can tell that the broth is made with a slow simmer that allows for rich and aromatic flavours, with subtle spices that leave you wanting more. 

A taste of Hanoi in Copenhagen I love sitting on one of the street tables in summer and enjoying a bowl of hot pho.
