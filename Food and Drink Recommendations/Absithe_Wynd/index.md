# The Last Tuesday Society & Absinthe Parlour
This bar is centred around absinthe, with carefully curated cocktails designed for the four different absinthes that each have a different botanical combination. Everything on the menu tastes wonderful and is a joy to drink, I would highly recommend it if you have not had the opportunity to try absinthe before and are curios if you like it. 

The atmosphere of the bar is also amazingly curated, with a wonderfully dark and occult collection of trinkets and object that make you as thought you are part of a secret society, hidden away from the busy streets outside and making decisions that impact the development of the world from the shadowy dark interior. This wonderful atmosphere is continued into the basement of the bar, where you will find the Victor Wynd Museum of Curiosities, an eclectic collection of occulture and the esoteric. It does require a ticket to enter, but if you are a fan of the occult I highly recommend making your way down there and spending some time looking at what they have to offer.
